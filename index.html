What is BMI ?

Body mass index which is shorted to BMI, is a measure of body fat based on weight and height of both, men and women. To calculate your BMI, you need to use a simple formula which is BMI=kg/m2. which Kg stands for the person's weight in kilograms, and m2 is the height in meters squared.
