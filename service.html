What we provide

PROGRAMS

We offer a wide variety of programs. Lifestyle Transformations, Bootcamps, Swim clinics, Small Group Training, 12 weeks intensive learning, Teen Workout Programs & Classes, On-Site Training and more!

PERSONAL TRAINING

We have two professional trainers on staff to help you reach your goals! We analyze your goals and needs, and create a plan tailored to you. Our personal trainers will help you to perform excercises with correct techniques.

PHYSIOTHERAPY

We have a physio at GETFIT, who is specialist not only in excercise prescription, but in human movement as well. You can seek advice of our physio in order to prevent the injuries that may occur after trying a new fitness routine.
